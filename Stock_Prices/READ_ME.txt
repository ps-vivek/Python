The stock prices for the company concerned was extracted from yahoo finance. 
The extracted stock details were split randomly across 20 files after adding few additional details as noise.

Stocks.py script parses around 20 files in a directory that contain the stock prices and other details related to a company.
With the processed stock prices , following details related to stock of a company are obtained.
1) Maximum stock price 
2) Maximum stock price Date 
3) Minimum stock price 
4) Minimum stock price date
